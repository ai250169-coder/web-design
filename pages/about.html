About Scramblet Store

We believe every reader deserves easy access to great books

📚

Our Story

Scramblet Store was born from a simple frustration — great books are everywhere, but buying them conveniently in Malaysia is not always easy. Physical bookstores have limited stock, inconvenient hours, and not everyone lives near one.

We set out to build a platform where anyone, from students to working professionals, can browse, discover, and purchase books online — anytime, from any device.

  • Accessible to readers of all ages (13 and above)
  • Works smoothly on both mobile and desktop
  • Curated selection — no fake or low-quality products
  • Fast, simple checkout with transparent pricing
  • Advanced search and genre filters so you find what you need
🔭
Our Vision
To be a leading and inclusive online bookstore that provides diverse and accessible reading resources for all ages — inspiring knowledge, creativity, and lifelong learning in the digital era.
🎯
Our Mission
To develop an easy and convenient online platform where users can browse, search, and purchase books without going physically to a bookstore — available 24/7, from any device.
💡
The Problem We Solve
Many people in Malaysia lack the time or access to visit bookstores. Physical shops also have limited stock. We solve this by giving everyone a wide catalogue, anytime access, and reliable delivery.
🏆
Our Success Criteria
Users can easily find books with smart search and filters. Ordering is fast and simple. The design is attractive and user-friendly. The site works well on both mobile and desktop.
Who We Serve

Our Target Audience

Scramblet Store is designed for a diverse community of readers across Malaysia.

🎓
Students (Age 13–25)
Secondary school and university students looking for reference books, fiction, and self-development reads. Comfortable with smartphones and online shopping platforms.
💼
Working Professionals (25–45)
Busy adults from the M40 and B40 income groups who prefer the convenience of shopping online. They value fast delivery, clear pricing, and a trusted platform.
👨‍👩‍👧
Parents & Families
Parents seeking educational and recreational books for their children. They prioritise a safe, trustworthy platform with a curated, age-appropriate selection.
